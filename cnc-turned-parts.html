CNC Turned Parts

We have adopted the perfect system of machining of parts from varioius types of metarials on cnc machine. We have capacity of turning on cnc machine upto 300 mm of diameter, maximum length of 900 mm, 500 mm wide and 400 mm thick parts. We always get the closest result of accuracy as per drawing or customer requirement. Our setup of cnc machine can turned round parts, unshaped parts and even forged parts from numerous metals like cast iron, mild steel, alloy steel, non ferrous and other related metal as per customer requirements.
